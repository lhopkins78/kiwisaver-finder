How It Works

📊 Fee Impact First

Fees compound just like returns — but in reverse. A 1% fee vs 0.2% fee costs you hundreds of thousands over 30 years. We show this prominently.

🎯 Avoid Algorithm

Funds are flagged "Avoid" if: fee >1%, OR fee >0.8% and underperforming benchmark, OR active fund that has missed benchmark for 3+ years.

📈 Benchmark Comparison

Every fund is measured against a relevant index. Outperforming matters — but only after fees. A fund that beats benchmark by 0.3% but charges 1.2% is still losing you money vs a passive alternative.

🔒 No ESG Scoring

Pure financial performance. Fees, returns, and what you actually own. Nothing else.
